# Sports Betting Localization

## Overview
Large-scale EN–PT-BR localization project for sportsbook platforms targeting the Brazilian regulated market.

Content included betting markets, promotional materials, and user interface strings requiring high terminology precision and numerical accuracy.

---

## Project Scope
- 40,000+ words
- Dynamic betting markets
- Promotional banners
- UI strings and system messages

---

## Key Challenges

### Terminology Precision
Complex betting types required strict consistency across markets and promotional content.

### Numerical & Odds Formatting
Validation of numerical syntax, decimal standards, and betting logic to ensure alignment with Brazilian conventions.

### Regulatory Sensitivity
Content required linguistic alignment with Brazilian regulatory expectations for gambling-related platforms.

---

## Localization Approach

### 1. Terminology Governance
- Glossary development and validation
- Standardization of betting market terminology
- Cross-reference checks across related market types

### 2. Structured Localization
- Context-aware EN–PT-BR adaptation
- Cultural alignment for Brazilian audience
- UI constraint awareness (character limits, formatting)

### 3. Quality Controls Applied
- Terminology consistency verification
- Numerical logic cross-checking
- Structural review for UI integrity
- Final Linguistic QA validation

---

## Tools Used
- MemoQ
- SDL Trados
- Translation Memory systems
- Terminology databases

---

## Outcome

Delivered terminology-consistent, culturally adapted localization aligned with Brazilian regulatory and market conventions.

Ensured usability, numerical accuracy, and regulatory compliance across dynamic sportsbook environments.
